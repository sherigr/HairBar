About Us

the hair and color bar is a boutique salon located in the Upper East Side and has been dedicated to delivering head-turning styles and color to the discerning clientele of New York City for years.  We offer a complete line of hair services, and specialize in hair color. All Stylist are trained in the latest coloring and styling techniques and we use only the highest quality professional products.


We are a New York-owned and operated business that takes pride in our city, and we show it in everything we do. Whether you need a fresh coloring or a new style, we will make sure you leave our salon feeling confident and beautiful. Call us for an appointment today!
